About Me

Marrel is an impressionist who is known for experimenting with different media. A Hotel and Restaurant Management graduate, Marrel has always wanted to be a full-time artist, but today juggles three main responsibilities: as a father, tour driver and member of the Yaru nu Artes Ivatan. Despite competing priorities and financial considerations, Marrel has made time to practise his art, which he says has been his main channel for easing his anxieties and expressing his emotions.
